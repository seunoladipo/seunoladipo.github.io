GIS & mapping projects

Gran Canaria, Spain
CO₂ Sink Suitability Analysis — Macaronesian Islands
Multi-criteria GIS land suitability model identifying optimal afforestation and reforestation sites across Gran Canaria, integrating soil, slope, climate, and land cover data. Analysed greenhouse gas emissions across all 8 Canary Islands, identifying three major emission clusters. Results published in a Q1 journal.
ArcGIS Pro QGIS Weighted Overlay Raster Analysis
Published StoryMaps ↗
Osun State, Nigeria
Geospatial Distribution of Sawmills — Ife-East LGA
Spatial mapping and analysis of informal sawmill locations in Ife-East Local Government Area. Identified clustering patterns and proximity to forest reserves to support deforestation management policy. Field survey data integrated with GIS modelling.
QGIS ArcMap Google Earth Engine Field Survey
Published
Anaga Massif, Tenerife
Phytotoponym Mapping — Historical Plant Community Reconstruction
Spatial analysis of phytotoponyms (plant place names) in Anaga Massif as proxies for reconstructing historical vegetation distributions and lost plant communities. Contributed to a conference paper at the XVIII Spanish Geography Congress.
ArcGIS Pro Spatial Statistics Historical Maps
Conference
Groningen, Netherlands · WWF
Urban Green Space Mapping — City of Groningen
Collaborated with WWF Netherlands and the municipality of Groningen to map existing urban green spaces, assess their ecosystem service provision potential, and develop a spatially informed plan for strategic green infrastructure expansion.
QGIS OpenStreetMap Stakeholder Mapping

Northern Nigeria · CESRA
Deforestation & Forest Degradation Mapping
Developed detailed maps of deforestation and forest degradation across Northern Nigeria during a GIS Specialist internship at the Centre for Space Research and Application. Digitised forest reserve boundaries and created photogrammetry-based change detection reports for national monitoring.

Land Suitability
Gran Canaria
CO₂ Sink Suitability Analysis — Macaronesian Islands
Multi-criteria GIS suitability model identifying optimal afforestation and reforestation sites across Gran Canaria, integrating soil, slope, climate, land cover, and carbon emission data. Results published in Trees, Forests and People (Q1).

Spatial Distribution
Osun, Nigeria
Geospatial Distribution of Sawmills — Ife-East LGA
Mapped and analysed informal sawmill locations across Ife-East Local Government Area, Osun State. Identified spatial clustering patterns, proximity to forest reserves, and deforestation risk zones — informing forest management policy recommendations.
QGIS ArcMap Google Earth Engine Field Survey

Historical Mapping
Anaga, Tenerife
Phytotoponym Mapping — Anaga Massif, Canary Islands
Identified and spatially analysed phytotoponyms (plant place names) in Anaga Massif, Tenerife, using them as proxies to reconstruct historical plant distributions and lost vegetation communities. Contributed to a conference paper at the XVIII Spanish Geography Congress.
ArcGIS Pro Historical Maps Spatial Statistics

Urban Green Spaces
Groningen, NL
Green Space Mapping — City of Groningen
Collaborated with WWF Netherlands and the municipality of Groningen to map urban green spaces, assess their ecosystem service provision, and develop a plan for strategic green infrastructure expansion. Used spatial data to prioritise intervention areas.
QGIS OpenStreetMap Stakeholder Mapping
Deforestation Tracking
Northern Nigeria
Deforestation & Forest Degradation Mapping — Northern Nigeria
During a GIS Specialist internship at CESRA, developed detailed maps of deforestation and forest degradation across Northern Nigeria using QGIS and ArcGIS. Digitised forest reserve boundaries and created photogrammetry-based change detection reports to support national monitoring.
ArcGIS QGIS Photogrammetry Change Detection
Ecosystem Restoration
Nigeria
Forest Inventory & Ecosystem Restoration GIS — FRIN
As Research Scientist at the Forestry Research Institute of Nigeria, oversaw collection and spatial analysis of forest inventory data from over 20 sites. Contributed to regional forestry management plans and developed a climate and forestry knowledge portal.

About Me

I am an environmental scientist with a strong background in forestry, spatial science, and GIS-based environmental analysis. My academic and professional work focuses on understanding how forest and landscape management strategies can enhance biodiversity, ecosystem services, and resilience under climate change.

Research Interests

Forest adaptation and climate resilience
Biodiversity responses to environmental change
Ecosystem services and trade-off analysis
GIS and remote sensing for environmental modelling

Selected Projects

Land Suitability Analysis for Afforestation

Developed a GIS-based land suitability model for afforestation and reforestation in Gran Canaria, Spain, assessing ecological and climatic constraints to support climate mitigation strategies.

Forest Adaptation Trade-offs

Evaluated biodiversity and ecosystem service trade-offs across alternative forest adaptation approaches under projected climate scenarios.
